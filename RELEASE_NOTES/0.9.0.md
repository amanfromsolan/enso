## New
- Light mode is here. Enso's chrome follows your Mac's appearance; the terminal keeps your Ghostty theme.
- Switch from the command palette ("Toggle Light/Dark Mode" or "Change Appearance") or Settings → Appearance. Your choice is remembered.
- The ⌘T palette is redesigned: Suggestions, Recent Tabs, and Commands sections, folder context on tabs, Enter opens a new tab.
- The Ctrl-Tab switcher matches the palette, appears instantly, and shows your nine most recent tabs.

## Improved
- What's New and the space editor share a new frosted look with rounder corners.
- Crisper typography and bigger icons across the sidebar and palettes.

## Fixed
- Full screen no longer shows a blank bar at the top; the sidebar toggle stays reachable at the top-left.
- Switching to an empty space no longer flashes stale terminal content.
- Folder collapse animation no longer desyncs icons from labels.
- The cursor no longer sticks as a text I-beam over buttons in the space editor.
- Removed the notification settings that didn't do anything yet.
