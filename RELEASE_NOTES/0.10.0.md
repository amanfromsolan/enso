## New
- Tabs running claude or codex pick up where they left off after a relaunch.
- Tabs show an icon for what's running: Claude, Codex, and more.
- The terminal header now sports a big icon for whichever agent is clanking away on your codebase.
- New tabs open in the current tab's folder.
- Open the changelog from the command palette.
- Meet Enso Next, our early-birds build. It runs alongside Enso and gets the cool features first, but can be unstable at times. Download: github.com/amanfromsolan/enso/releases/tag/next

## Improved
- Rename a tab inline by double-clicking its title.
- Sidebar icons have been refreshed and work across light and dark mode.
- Collapsed folders show the active tab.
