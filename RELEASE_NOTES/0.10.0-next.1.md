## New
- Welcome to Enso Next — the pre-release channel. It runs alongside regular Enso with its own icon and its own settings, and updates ahead of everyone else.
- Agent sessions survive relaunch: tabs running claude or codex resume their conversation right where you left off. Quitting still stops the agents; nothing runs behind your back.
- Fresh icon set — new artwork for Enso, Enso Next, and dev builds so you can tell them apart in the Dock at a glance.

## Improved
- Dev builds are now called "Enso Dev" (previously "Enso Nightly"); your existing dev state carries over automatically.
